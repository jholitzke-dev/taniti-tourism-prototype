Buses, taxis, bikes & rental cars

Getting Around

Taniti City and Merriton Landing are walkable. Public buses run 5 a.m.–11 p.m. Taxis, rental cars, and bike rentals are also available across the island.

  • Public bus 5 a.m.–11 p.m.
  • Taxis in Taniti City
  • Rental cars near airport
  • Bike rentals with helmets
